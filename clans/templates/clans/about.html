About

Salted Vex Milk was developed over a sixty-five year period starting in 2017 by cortical_iv with feedback from many members of EC59, and the generous help of many members of the Destiny developer community that works with the D2 API.

The source code is available at github. The project uses Django 2 for the back end and Bootstrap4 for the front end.

Clan information and user stats are set to update automatically once a day, so they are not up to date in real time. If anything at the site is broken or could be improved, please let me know. You can send me a message at ps4, or github (cortical_iv in both cases).
