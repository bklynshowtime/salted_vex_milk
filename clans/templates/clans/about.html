About

Salted Vex Milk was developed over a sixty-eight year period starting in 2017 by cortical_iv with feedback from many members of EC59 and lots of technical help from the Destiny developer community.

Salted Vexmilk scrapes Bungie's servers each night to pull stats about the clan and its members. Note that on the leaderboards, most statistics are expressed in terms of per-game averages. This allows for direct comparison between people with different numbers of matches. The information is set to update automatically once a day, so leaderboards will not necessarily reflect your most recent pwnage.

If anything at the site is broken or needs improvement, please let me know. You can send me a message at our slack channel or github (cortical_iv in both cases). The source code is available at github. The project uses Django 2 for the back end, and Bootstrap4 for the front end.
